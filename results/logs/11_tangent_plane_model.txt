Experiment 11 — Tangent Plane Model

Definitions:
x_plane = theta - 45 degrees
y_plane = S_tangent - S_normal

Balance point:
theta_balance        = 45.000000000000
phi_balance          = 45.000000000000
x_balance            = 0.000000000000
y_balance            = 0.000000000000
distance_balance     = 0.000000000000000000e+00

Interpretation:
The orthogonal trace can be represented as a tangent-plane coordinate system centered at the isotropic axis. At theta=45 degrees, normal and tangent components balance and the tangent-plane trace crosses the origin. This reconstructs the tangent plane as a geometric consequence of orthogonal projection, not as an independent assumption.
