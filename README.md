## Overview

The GEO Object Reconstruction Lab reconstructs a Coupled Geometric Architecture (CGA) from recurrent geometric signatures and progressively recovered structural constraints.

The laboratory was designed to answer a simple question:

> What geometric object is implied by the recurrent signatures 3/4, √(3/5), and π/4?

Rather than introducing a predefined model, the reconstruction proceeds through a sequence of audited experiments that progressively recover conservation laws, coupling behavior, bifocal structure, orthogonal projection, tangent-plane emergence, and internal operator relationships.

The objective of this repository is the reconstruction and audit of the geometric object itself.

This repository does not require cosmological, gravitational, or vacuum interpretations in order to reproduce the reconstructed architecture.
